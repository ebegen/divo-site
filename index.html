What's inside

🌙

Going Out Mode

Flip a switch when you're heading out. Divo prioritises matches at the same venue or area, tonight only.

👯

Group Going Out

Build a squad of 2–4 friends, match with another squad, meet up as a group. Less awkward, more fun.

🎙️

Voice Intros

30-second voice clip on every profile. Personality lands before the first message — no more text-blind matches.

🤖

AI Coach

On-demand coach reads your match's profile and helps you write the first message that actually gets a reply.

🧾

Compatibility Receipt

Every match comes with a 9:16 receipt — values, vibe, music, dealbreakers — so you know why you're a fit.

↩️

Replay & Rewind

Swiped too fast? Bring back the last profile and give yourself a second look.

✈️

Travel Mode

Heading to another city? Switch your match radius for the trip and connect with locals before you land.

🛡️

Safety Center

Pre-meet check-in shares your plan with a trusted contact. One tap to share location, one tap to alert.

🎁

Reward Reveal

Streaks, milestones, in-app perks — small wins that make the journey lighter, not gamier.

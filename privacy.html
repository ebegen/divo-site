Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

At Divo, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our dating application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing).
  • Profile Information: Your name, date of birth, gender, bio, city, occupation, education, interests, personality traits, and relationship intent.
  • Photos: Profile photos you upload and photos shared within chat conversations.
  • Location Data: If you choose to share your location in chat, we process your approximate location. This is optional and requires your explicit consent.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app (swipes, matches, messages) to help us improve the experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your dating profile.
  • To show you relevant profiles based on your preferences.
  • To facilitate matches and enable real-time messaging between matched users.
  • To maintain safety through report and block features.
  • To improve and maintain the app's functionality and performance.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers using industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest). We implement strict access controls and regularly audit our security measures. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Camera, Photo Library & Microphone

We request access to your device's camera, photo library, and microphone for the following purposes:

  • Camera: To take photos for your profile and to share in chat conversations.
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for your profile and chat.
  • Microphone: For voice messages and video features within chat.
  • Location: To optionally share your location with matches in chat.

These permissions are only requested when you use the relevant features, and you can revoke them at any time through your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your profile information is visible only to other registered users of Divo. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Delete your account, which will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
  • Opt out of non-essential data processing.

Children's Privacy

Divo is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a minor, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and on this page.
